Team Personas: Student Transportation

Barry West: A 23 year old male who is currently obtaining his Master's. While he does not live too far from campus, he struggles to find a safe and convienent way to get around.

Team Storyboards: Campus Transportation

Despite living near campus for over a year, Barry constantly finds himself without a reliable source of transportation, as he also doesn't own a car. What he needs is an efficient method of getting around campus without stressing over the bus. Luckily, his roommate, Davis, has just the solution, a brand new app that allows students to rent a method of transportation! The next week, Barry has his own personal scooter! He can go all around campus now without having to worry about when or where the bus is going!
